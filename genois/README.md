# Genois

`genois/` is the local training and ingest workspace for Stell.

Purpose:
- stage approved documents before indexing
- separate inbox from approved material
- keep archive and manifest records predictable

Folders:
- `inbox/`: newly imported or unreviewed content
- `approved/`: content ready for indexing
- `archive/`: retired or superseded content
- `manifests/`: sync and checksum metadata
